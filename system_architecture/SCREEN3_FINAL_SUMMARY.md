### **Component 2: Participation Over Time Chart**
- **Type:** Line/Area chart
- **Shows:** When students took quiz, participation peaks, engagement timeline
- **X-Axis:** Time (12am, 3am, 6am... or relative)
- **Y-Axis:** Number of students

---

### **Component 3: Score Distribution Chart**
- **Type:** Histogram/Bar chart
- **Shows:** How scores spread across all participants
- **Color Coding:** 🔴 Red (Poor), 🟡 Yellow (Average), 🟢 Green (Good)
- **Interactive:** Click to see students in each range

```

```

### **Entry Point 2: From Quiz Manager**
```
Screen8 (Quiz Manager)
  ↓
Click [View] button on any quiz
  ↓
→ Screen3 (Quiz Dashboard)
```

### **Entry Point 3: After Quiz Completion**
```
Student takes quiz
  ↓
Clicks [Submit Quiz]
  ↓
→ Screen3 (Shows Results)
```
